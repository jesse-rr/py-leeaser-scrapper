Overall 8.8/10

The main body of the story before chapter 350 is incredibly stable but once Da-In starts his "retirement", the story really starts to rush and hurry along, opening some plot holes. Only 2 plot holes come to mind the first and most main story plot significant is the time reversed being effected by the BBG's insanity even tho he had powers from the same source as Da-In and Haru, meaning he should really be immune to the insanity. Some may argue that the BBG targeted the time reverser, but it is then stated later that there is a shield that prevents interference around the world. The premise of the side story starts with a plot hole, that the star god who died earlier in like chapter 400ish told Da-In that the Sun god who died 40ish chapters later is dead in another world. Then died her self. The timelines don't match up. These plot holes dropped the story from 9 star to 8.8. It lost a full star for an empty ending. I need an after story about the egostream life without endless chaos, or even better, a different after story for when Haru (who becomes a god) out lives everyone around her. How does she solve that? How does she cope with it? These are important questions for me, who has finished the story.
